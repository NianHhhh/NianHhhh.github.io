Functional Descriptions:

  • ZmTPS1, a gene encoding a terpene synthase, is among the most likely candidates for the mQTL on chromosome 2.
  • A previous study has shown that ZmTPS1 could catalyze the formation of linalool in vitro.
  • ZmTPS1 is involved in terpenoid biosynthesis and might have a significant impact on other metabolite pathways.
  • These results suggest that ZmTPS1 is involved in terpenoid biosynthesis and might have a significant impact on other metabolite pathways.
  • Given the role of ZmTPS1 in catalyzing the formation of linalool, we speculate that ZmTPS1 may play an important role in regulating the divergence in linalool between teosinte and tropical maize.
